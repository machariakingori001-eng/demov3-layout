Guide to video production costs

The cost of creating a film project depends on the complexity and resources involved.

Up to £5,000
Short well-crafted films can be produced at this budget level. Examples include event films, branded content & social media promos. Usually these involve a small crew.

£5,000 - £15,000

Our Workflow

The Production Process

Discovery & Consultation

Most projects start with an initial consultation. This is where we listen to your vision, understand your brand voice, and define the core objectives of the video.

  • Creative Briefing
  • Goal Definition
  • Budget Alignment
  • Strategic Input

Conceptual Development

Once we agree to move forward, we refine the project concept. We develop scripts, mood boards, and visual styles to ensure we are perfectly aligned before a camera starts rolling.

  • Scriptwriting
  • Storyboarding
  • Visual Style Guide
  • Location Scouting

Pre-Production Planning

The logistics phase. We handle casting, crew scheduling, equipment rental, and permits. This ensures the shoot day runs flawlessly and on schedule.

  • Cast & Crew Hiring
  • Scheduling
  • Risk Assessment
  • Logistics Management

Filming & Production

Action. Our award-winning directors and cinematographers bring the vision to life using cinema-grade equipment, ensuring every frame looks premium.

  • 4K/6K Cinema Cameras
  • Professional Lighting
  • Directing Talent
  • On-set Audio

Post-Production Editing

Where the magic happens. We edit, color grade, sound design, and add motion graphics to create a polished final product ready for distribution.

  • Video Editing
  • Color Grading
  • Sound Design
  • VFX & Motion Graphics
